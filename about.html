GALLERY MENU ABOUT CONTACT

We are a boutique cupcakery, located in the heart of Wellington on Courtney Place. Our cupcakes are made with love and care, fresh every morning to satisfy your sweet tooth. Ingredients are sourced from our local markets here in Wellington, which results with a moist cake that is topped off with a perfect ratio, butter smooth, hand-piped icing that is light and fluffy and full with lots of flavour.

We have the perfect cupcake for you, whether that is a birthday party, social gatherings or your just picking up something sweet for morning or afternoon tea, we offer a wide range of cake and icing flavours for everyone, we also offer Gluten free options on some of our cupcake ranges, (All GF options will have a surplus charge of $1.00).

We also create custom made flavours and colours too for special events (increase charges may be included for these requests). If you are looking for a larger order please get in contact with our staff at our cupcake store. All our cupcake orders are in-store pickup only, no delivery available.
